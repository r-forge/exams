Exam 1

  1. Question

    For the matrix
    A =( 16416-4 456-9 16633-28 -4-9-2858 ).

    compute the matrix L=( ℓij )1≤i,j≤4 from the Cholesky decomposition A=L L⊤ .
    Which of the following statements are true?
    1. ℓ41 =-1
    2. ℓ44 <4
    3. ℓ22 =-1
    4. ℓ11 >0
    5. ℓ32 ≤1

    Solution

    The decomposition yields
    L =( 4000 1200 4140 -1-4-54 )

    and hence:
    1. True. ℓ41 =-1
    2. False. ℓ44 =4≮4
    3. False. ℓ22 =2≠-1
    4. True. ℓ11 =4
    5. True. ℓ32 =1
