Exam 1

  1. Question

    The daily expenses of summer tourists in Vienna are analyzed. A survey with 71 tourists is conducted. This shows that the tourists spend on average 130 EUR. The sample variance sn-1 2 is equal to 83.2.
    Determine a 95% confidence interval for the average daily expenses (in EUR) of a tourist.

    1. What is the lower confidence bound?
    2. What is the upper confidence bound?

    Solution

    The 95% confidence interval for the average expenses μ is given by:
    [ y ‾  - 1.96 sn-1 2 n ,   y ‾  + 1.96 sn-1 2 n ] = [130 - 1.96 83.2 71 ,  130 + 1.96 83.2 71 ] = [127.878, 132.122].


    1. The lower confidence bound is 127.878.
    2. The upper confidence bound is 132.122.
