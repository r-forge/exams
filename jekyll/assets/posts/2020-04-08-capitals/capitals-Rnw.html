Exam 1

  1. Question

    Which of the following cities are the capital of the corresponding country?

    1. Warsaw (Poland)
    2. Auckland (New Zealand)
    3. New Delhi (India)
    4. Zürich (Switzerland)
    5. São Paulo (Brazil)

    Solution


    1. True. Warsaw is the capital of Poland.
    2. False. The capital of New Zealand is Wellington.
    3. True. New Delhi is the capital of India.
    4. False. The de facto capital of Switzerland is Bern.
    5. False. The capital of Brazil is Brasilia.
