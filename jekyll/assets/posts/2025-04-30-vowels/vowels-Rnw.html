Exam 1

  1. Question

    Describe the following Cardinal vowels according to the three parameters vowel height, frontness-backness, and lip rounding:
    1. e is the close / near-close / close-mid / mid / open-mid / near-open / open front / front centralized / central / back centralized / back unrounded / rounded vowel.
    2. ɶ is the close / near-close / close-mid / mid / open-mid / near-open / open front / front centralized / central / back centralized / back unrounded / rounded vowel.


    Solution

    For an overview of the 18 Cardinal vowels see https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cardinal_vowels.
    1. e is the close-mid front unrounded vowel.
    2. ɶ is the open front rounded vowel.
