    What is the distance between the two points p=(3,4)p = (3, 4) and q=(5,2)q = (5, 2) in a Cartesian coordinate system?


    1. 4.0584.058
    2. 2.0002.000
    3. 0.8270.827
    4. 2.8282.828
    5. 4.8484.848

    Solution

    The distance dd of pp and qq is given by d2=(p1−q1)2+(p2−q2)2d^2 = (p_1 - q_1)^2 + (p_2 - q_2)^2 (Pythagorean formula).

    Hence d=(p1−q1)2+(p2−q2)2=(3−5)2+(4−2)2=2.828d = \sqrt{(p_1 - q_1)^2 + (p_2 - q_2)^2} = \sqrt{(3 - 5)^2 + (4 - 2)^2} = 2.828.

    3. False
