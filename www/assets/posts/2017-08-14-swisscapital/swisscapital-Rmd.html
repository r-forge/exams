Exam 1

  1. Question

    What is the seat of the federal authorities in Switzerland (i.e., the de facto capital)?


    1. St. Gallen
    2. Zurich
    3. Lausanne
    4. Bern
    5. Geneva

    Solution

    There is no de jure capital but the de facto capital and seat of the federal authorities is Bern.


    1. False
    2. False
    3. False
    4. True
    5. False
