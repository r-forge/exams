Exam 1

  1. Question

    For the matrix
    A =( 16-12-12-16 -12251-4 -1211714 -16-41457 ).

    compute the matrix L=( ℓij )1≤i,j≤4 from the Cholesky decomposition A=L L⊤ .
    Which of the following statements are true?
    1. ℓ41 ≥-4
    2. ℓ33 ≥2
    3. ℓ11 ≤4
    4. ℓ31 ≥-3
    5. ℓ32 ≥-2

    Solution

    The decomposition yields
    L =( 4000 -3400 -3-220 -4-4-34 )

    and hence:
    1. True. ℓ41 =-4
    2. True. ℓ33 =2
    3. True. ℓ11 =4
    4. True. ℓ31 =-3
    5. True. ℓ32 =-2
